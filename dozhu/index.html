Learn Mandarin from Taiwan

Dozhu is a language learning app aimed for those who wish to learn Taiwanese Mandarin, featuring Traditional characters and the Zhuyin phonetic system.


These, in turn, are the standard variation to just Taiwan, as Mainland China uses simplified hanzi and Pinyin transliteration.


Top reasons to choose Taiwanese Mandarin:


Intact Radicals

They provide more intuitive and efficient learning curve for character reading and writing on the long run.

Movies, books, tv and games

Due to the absence of media blockage, Western productions in general are readily available through Traditional Mandarin.

Same Internet

Traditional Mandarin speakers post on the same Instagram, Twitter, Tiktok and Reddit as Westerners do, allowing for greater interaction - a key element in language learning!

Easier through Japanese

More similarities to the Japanese language are shared with Taiwanese style over Mainland's, and most Japanese turn to Taiwan's method due to this proximity.
