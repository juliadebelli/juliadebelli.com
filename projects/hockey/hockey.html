Summary

Blocky Hockey: All Stars is a mobile game by Canadian Gogii Games in a partnership with Manifesto Games, a game studio I was interning at the time.

Credits to RRVirus for gameplay video

I worked with a senior 3D artist, a UI designer, and developers. The project had already begun when I joined ship and we already had our skater models ready.


My work

I delievered most of the 2D concept art. I drew them in isometric perspective using Photoshop. This is a sample of one of the pieces we sent to Gogii when we were validating landscape elements, track obstacles and interactive objects:

Later, I used 3DS Max for trying on textures on the models. I made textures for all characters (including animals)

With all textures approved, I animated the models to pose different and drew backgrounds for hockey stamps used in character selection.
