Problem Statement

Biciflow first came to be in 2018 as a class project at the Center of Informatics (UFPE).

I wasn't initially part of the team responsible for its first launch. 5 years later, I was called in to contribute with user interaction following a repurposed business model.

My job was designing a new app that was now focused on rewarding users for cycling to a sports or music venue. This study encompasses the 2023 re-design, and is going to be updated when later versions are developed and released.



Research Phase

User Research

In 2022, a concierge-MVP test was run by Pedro and Igor, in which they stood by the entrance to Wehoo Festival (local music event). Cyclists would arrive at the location, and would show their phones with a Strava activity opened. If the trip began farther away from Wehoo and finished close by, along with a series of other validators that are trade-secrets even to myself, a discount would be issued to the user.

Over 110 attendees were rewarded. The initiative was highly praised on social media, and asked to be replicated in similar events to come.
